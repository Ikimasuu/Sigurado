My proof of residence is under my landlord's name. Will that work? Ang proof of residence ko ay nasa pangalan ng landlord ko. Pwede ba 'yon?
Yes, that's fine. Brgy. San Roque accepts Meralco or water bills even if they're under a landlord or housemate's name — as long as the address matches. Just bring it alongside your valid ID. Oo, okay lang. Tinatanggap ng Brgy. San Roque ang Meralco o water bills kahit nasa pangalan ng landlord o kasama — basta tugma ang address. Dalhin mo lang kasama ng valid ID mo.
